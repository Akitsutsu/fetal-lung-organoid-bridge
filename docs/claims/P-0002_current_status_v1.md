# P-0002 current status v1

## Scope
This is a current-state claim memo in the new OS.
It is not a closure note, not a prediction-registry replacement, and not a comparison-world recreation.

## Current claim frame
P-0002 states that organoid developmental programs can be aligned to fetal lung alveolarization-relevant developmental programs without reducing interpretation to generic nearest-state mapping alone. The claim is registered under the `alveolarization_alignment` program. Strict success requires alignment coherent across stage, trajectory, morphology, and off-target axes, and remaining stable when morphology, temporal structure, and image-linked or spatial evidence are considered.

## Current support
Five `claim_links = P-0002` support rows now exist. They are partial-axis and complementary. EV-0009 is a derived `unit_level = serial_chain` unit over GSM6858852 and GSM6858853 (BU3_NGAT iAT1 ALI p0 -> p1), carrying support along the trajectory (SOX2lowCFTR+ strengthens p0 -> p1), stage coherence (late_GW17_19 sharpens), and quality (off-target decreases) sub-axes, with culture format held constant at ALI. EV-0010 is a derived sample-level assessed interpretation built on EV-0008 (GSM8217894 GW18 Xenium) under `source_unit_id = GSM8217894_analysis_v1`; it carries support on the spatial-architecture sub-axis in native fetal lung at a matching developmental stage. The SOX2lowCFTR+ trajectory state shows a coherent distal-epithelial niche (spatial correlation r = 0.85 with distal_epi across 2,147 valid 200 um bins), together with expected regional structure for airway and budtip populations.

EV-0011 is a derived image-set interpretation built on GSM8217894 morphology-linked cell segmentation under `source_unit_id = GSM8217894_morphology_axis_v1`; it carries conservative morphology-linked tissue-architecture support. In the morphology-axis v1 analysis, SOX2low/distal enriched 200 um bins retained cell-mask occupancy close to the measured-bin distribution (median cell-mask fraction 0.825 vs 0.829 across measured bins) and did not show inflated single-empty-component structure (median largest empty component fraction 0.078 vs 0.094 across measured bins), arguing against the EV-0010 distal niche being a morphology-empty edge artifact.

EV-0013 is a derived sample-level assessed interpretation built on EV-0012 (GSM8217893 GW15 Xenium) under `source_unit_id = GSM8217893_analysis_v1`; it carries same-series cross-stage spatial support. The GW15 base SOX2lowCFTR+ class has elevated proximal-airway co-correlation (Pearson distal 0.811, proximal 0.623; Spearman distal 0.835, proximal 0.819), so the durable interpretation does not rely on the base class alone. Stricter SFTPC-positive variants preserve the distal-epithelial anchoring while reducing proximal association: `base_plus_sftpc` has Pearson distal 0.723 and proximal 0.090, and `base_exclude_proximal_plus_sftpc` has Pearson distal 0.636 and proximal -0.039. After budtip-progenitor exclusion, the GW15 SFTPC-positive signal remains distal-biased over proximal (`base_plus_sftpc_exclude_budtip`: Pearson distal 0.710, proximal 0.145, budtip 0.663), supporting a signal that is not solely budtip-driven while retaining an explicit distal/budtip developmental-continuity caveat.

EV-0014 is a derived image-set interpretation built on GSM8217893 morphology-linked cell segmentation under `source_unit_id = GSM8217893_morphology_axis_v1`; it carries conservative morphology-linked artifact-exclusion and tissue-embeddedness support for the GW15 same-series stage. In the stricter-variant morphology-axis analysis, SFTPC-positive SOX2low/distal enriched bins retained segmentation-mask tissue occupancy (median cell-mask fraction 0.883) without inflated largest-empty-component structure (median 0.040) relative to the GW15 epithelial baseline (0.865 and 0.063). The proximal-marker-excluded SFTPC SOX2low/distal variant behaved similarly (median cell-mask fraction 0.884 and largest-empty-component fraction 0.039). This supports limited artifact exclusion, not manual airspace geometry or histology-grade morphology.

As a non-independent consistency check only, the Codex-derived stricter SOX2lowCFTR+/SFTPC+ variants fall predominantly in the authors' processed broad epithelial cell_type compartment (0.85-0.89 in both GW15 and GW18) and form coherent subregions on the authors' processed UMAP embedding. This is consistency within a non-independent re-analysis of the same expression matrix rather than independent corroboration. EV-0010, EV-0011, EV-0013, and EV-0014 do not adjudicate AT1-maturity (HOPX, AQP5 absent from the 339-gene panel), mesenchymal off-target (VIM, COL1A1, COL3A1 absent), detailed manual airspace geometry, or independent cross-donor replication. Under the `alveolar_epithelial_maturation` scope_layer in `BIOLOGICAL_CONTRACT.yaml`, the remaining AT1-maturity gap should be treated as reflecting both panel-coverage limits and iPSC/hESC-derived organoid-system limits, rather than panel coverage alone. No single support row closes the full multimodal alignment condition of P-0002 on its own.

## Current bottom line
P-0002 now has five complementary claim-linked support rows. EV-0009 carries trajectory/stage/quality support on the organoid side. EV-0010 and EV-0013 carry spatial-architecture support on the fetal reference side at GW18 and GW15 respectively, with the GW15 row explicitly class-sensitive and primarily supported by SFTPC-positive stricter variants under a distal/budtip developmental-continuity caveat. EV-0011 and EV-0014 carry conservative morphology-linked artifact-exclusion and tissue-embeddedness support at GW18 and GW15 respectively. This partially addresses the prior cross-stage replication gap at the same-series level. Cross-donor replication, AT1-maturity, mesenchymal off-target adjudication, manual airspace geometry, and full multimodal closure remain absent.

## Next gap
Cross-donor replication in an independent fetal-lung Xenium or equivalent spatial dataset, and histology-grade or manual airspace/tissue-architecture annotation to convert the mask-based artifact-exclusion into biologically anchored morphology support. A stronger morphology-axis corroboration would directly review or measure raw morphology tiles at useful resolution to test whether the EV-0010 and EV-0013 distal-epithelial niches map to biologically interpretable distal lung morphology rather than only to morphology-derived cell-mask occupancy.
