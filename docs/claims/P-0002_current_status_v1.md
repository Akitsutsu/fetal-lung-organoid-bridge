# P-0002 current status v1

## Scope
This is a current-state claim memo in the new OS.
It is not a closure note, not a prediction-registry replacement, and not a comparison-world recreation.

## Current claim frame
P-0002 states that organoid developmental programs can be aligned to fetal lung alveolarization-relevant developmental programs without reducing interpretation to generic nearest-state mapping alone. The claim is registered under the `alveolarization_alignment` program. Strict success requires alignment coherent across stage, trajectory, morphology, and off-target axes, and remaining stable when morphology, temporal structure, and image-linked or spatial evidence are considered.

## Current support
Three `claim_links = P-0002` support rows now exist. They are partial-axis and complementary. EV-0009 is a derived `unit_level = serial_chain` unit over GSM6858852 and GSM6858853 (BU3_NGAT iAT1 ALI p0 → p1), carrying support along the trajectory (SOX2lowCFTR+ strengthens p0 → p1), stage coherence (late_GW17_19 sharpens), and quality (off-target decreases) sub-axes, with culture format held constant at ALI. EV-0010 is a derived sample-level assessed interpretation built on EV-0008 (GSM8217894 GW18 Xenium) under `source_unit_id = GSM8217894_analysis_v1`; it carries support on the spatial-architecture sub-axis in native fetal lung at a matching developmental stage — the SOX2lowCFTR+ trajectory state shows a coherent distal-epithelial niche (spatial correlation r = 0.85 with distal_epi across 2,147 valid 200 µm bins), together with expected regional structure for airway and budtip populations. EV-0011 is a derived image-set interpretation built on GSM8217894 morphology-linked cell segmentation under `source_unit_id = GSM8217894_morphology_axis_v1`; it carries conservative morphology-linked tissue-architecture support. In the morphology-axis v1 analysis, SOX2low/distal enriched 200 µm bins retained cell-mask occupancy close to the measured-bin distribution (median cell-mask fraction 0.825 vs 0.829 across measured bins) and did not show inflated single-empty-component structure (median largest empty component fraction 0.078 vs 0.094 across measured bins), arguing against the EV-0010 distal niche being a morphology-empty edge artifact. EV-0010 and EV-0011 do not adjudicate AT1-maturity (HOPX, AQP5 absent from the 339-gene panel), mesenchymal off-target (VIM, COL1A1, COL3A1 absent), detailed manual airspace geometry, or cross-donor replication. No single support row closes the full multimodal alignment condition of P-0002 on its own.

## Current bottom line
P-0002 now has three complementary claim-linked support rows. EV-0009 carries trajectory/stage/quality support on the organoid side; EV-0010 carries spatial-architecture support on the fetal reference side; EV-0011 adds the first conservative morphology-linked tissue-architecture support for the same GW18 fetal reference sample. This addresses the morphology axis at a limited artifact-exclusion and tissue-embeddedness level, not at the level of detailed alveolar airspace geometry. Cross-donor/cross-stage replication of the spatial and morphology-linked findings remains absent. Full multimodal closure is not claimed.

## Next gap

Cross-donor or cross-stage replication remains a separate gap.
